Data Retention

How long we keep your data and our deletion policies.

Retention Periods

We retain different types of data for different periods based on necessity:

Account Data

  • Active accounts: Retained while your account is active
  • Deleted accounts: Permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Inactive accounts: May be deleted after 2 years of inactivity

Message Data

  • Encrypted messages: Stored until you delete them
  • Auto-delete messages: Automatically removed based on your settings
  • Deleted messages: Permanently removed immediately

After Account Deletion

When you delete your account:

  • Your profile is immediately removed
  • Your messages are deleted from our servers
  • Some anonymized analytics data may be retained
  • Backup copies are purged within 30 days
