Introduction

At Menza, we believe privacy is a fundamental right. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our secure messaging platform.

üîí Privacy by Design

Menza is built with privacy at its core. We use end-to-end encryption (AES-256) to ensure that only you and your intended recipients can read your messages.

Quick Summary

Here's a quick overview of our privacy practices:

  • End-to-End Encryption: Your messages are encrypted and only readable by you and your recipients.
  • Minimal Data Collection: We only collect data necessary to provide our service.
  • No Message Logging: We cannot read or access the content of your encrypted messages.
  • Your Control: You can delete your data at any time.
  • No Selling: We never sell your personal information to third parties.
