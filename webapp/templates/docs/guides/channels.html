Using Channels

Create and manage broadcast channels for your audience.

What Are Channels?

Channels are one-to-many communication tools. Create a channel to broadcast messages to subscribers. Perfect for announcements, content creators, or communities.

  2. Select "New Channel"
  3. Choose a name and description

  5. Set visibility (Discoverable or Private)
  6. Invite members with roles (Admin, Mod, Viewer)

Channel Roles

  • Admin: Full control - manage members, settings, and posts
  • Moderator: Can post and comment, but can't change settings
  • Viewer: Read-only access to channel content

Discoverable vs Private

  • Discoverable: Appears in search and discovery feed
  • Private: Only accessible via invite link

Discoverable channels are ranked by engagement: likes, views, subscribers, and activity. Most popular channels appear first in discovery.

Channel Features

  • Rich text formatting (bold, italic, code)
  • Emoji reactions
  • Comments on posts
  • Like channels to support creators
  • Custom branding (emoji + colors)
