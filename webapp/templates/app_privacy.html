Privacy Policy

Menza uses end-to-end encryption (AES-256) for all messages. We cannot read your messages - only you and your intended recipients can.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Username, email address (optional), phone number (optional)
  • Profile Information: Display name, profile picture (optional)
  • Messages: Encrypted message content (we cannot read this)
  • Media: Photos, videos, and files you choose to share (encrypted)

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Usage Data: Features used, time spent in app (anonymized)
  • Crash Reports: Technical data to help us fix bugs
  • Push Tokens: For delivering notifications to your device

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Menza service
  • To deliver your messages to intended recipients
  • To send you notifications about new messages and calls
  • To improve our service and fix bugs
  • To protect against fraud and abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. End-to-End Encryption

What is encrypted:

  • All private messages between users
  • Group chat messages
  • Photos, videos, and files
  • Voice and video calls

What is NOT encrypted:

  • Your username and profile information (needed for others to find you)
  • Channel posts (public by design)
  • Message metadata (who you message, when - but not content)

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Messages are stored encrypted on our servers
  • Encryption keys are stored only on your device
  • We use industry-standard security measures
  • Data is stored in secure data centers
  • We conduct regular security audits

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share data only in these cases:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree
  • Service Providers: Companies that help us operate (bound by confidentiality)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights
  • Safety: To prevent harm or illegal activities

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics and non-essential data collection

7. Data Retention

  • Messages are retained until you delete them
  • Account data is kept while your account is active
  • Deleted accounts are permanently removed within 30 days
  • Backup copies may persist up to 90 days for disaster recovery

8. Children's Privacy

Menza is intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If we discover we have collected data from a minor, we will delete it immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this policy.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights including:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

12. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

European users have additional rights under GDPR including:

  • Right to access and data portability
  • Right to rectification and erasure
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent
