Bot Guidelines

Follow these guidelines to get your bot approved

Provide Clear Value

Your bot should solve a real problem or provide useful functionality. Price tracking, news alerts, moderation tools, and productivity bots are great examples.

âœ“

Respect User Privacy

Only request permissions you need. Don't store message content unless necessary. Have a clear privacy policy explaining data usage.

âœ“

Be Responsive

Respond to commands within 3 seconds. If processing takes longer, send an acknowledgment first then follow up with results.

âœ•

No Spam or Self-Promotion

Don't send unsolicited messages or ads. Only message users who have explicitly interacted with your bot or opted in.

âœ•

No Malicious Activity

Bots must not collect credentials, spread malware, impersonate other services, or engage in scams. Violations result in immediate suspension.

âœ•

No Harmful Content

Bots must not generate, promote, or facilitate access to illegal, violent, or sexually explicit content.
